About Me

Hi there! My name is Charlotte, although I'm sure you already know that from seeing the HUGE name at the top of the page. I am currently a student at Staffordshire University, studying Computer Games Programming. I have already produced a game for a client before in a previous course I have completed. This game can be found in the Time and Tide museum in Great Yarmouth - or if you can't be bothered to drive halfway across the country, you can check a little more out about that here. I am incredibly passionate about games and in the future, wish to go into either AI programming or Gameplay programming. I have always loved games such as The Sims, games by Frontier and other simulation games that I can play, because hey, we're all living in a simulation anyway. My ultimate dream is to work on a game that is the driving force towards discussing many taboo topics through the use of gaming, such as mental health, or why there isn't a Fable 2 remake yet... I hope you enjoy your stay on my site. If you wish to contact me, I suggest you click on any of my socials in the top right of any page on this site. Thank you!
